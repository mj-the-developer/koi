Easy Homemade Latte Recipe

Vida Lamakani March 10, 2021 1 Comment

Key Ingredients and Tools Necessary

Coffee: Espresso or strongly brewed coffee is essential for an authentic latte. Decaffeinated works as well.

Milk: Any variety you like. Skim milk, whole milk, reduced-fat. Or for a Dairy-Free/Vegan Latte use non-dairy milk such as almond, coconut, oat, or soy. You can even use a flavored creamer sweeter taste.

You only need one simple tool to make a delicious and frothy latte--a small kitchen whisk that fits inside your coffee mug. No fancy equipment is necessary! We prefer an aluminum whisk over silicone.
